Photography

Illustrator

Out of all the Adobe programs I've been using Illustrator for the longest. I was first introduced to Ilustrator in my junior year of high school and I immediately fell in love with the program. Photoshop

InDesign

I've only started using InDesign in the past two years and I've grown very familiar with the program. Not only can I use InDesign to create gloss layerd posters, brochures, and catalogs, but I can use InDesign to create interactive ads and simple flash games too!
